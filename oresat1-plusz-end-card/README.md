# OreSat1 +Z End Card

- See also README-fabrication.md and README-assembly.md

## Overview

This card sits in Slot 1 in the OreSat backplane and:

- Connects to the backplane with a main connector and two RF
- Connects four +Z solar modules
- Mounts and connects to the S band helical antenna
- Routes an SMPM connector to a U.FL connector to jump up to the +Z End Cap's S band patch antenna
- Has an OPD circuit to control:
   - Deploying the helical antenna
   - Switching C3-UART to any of the +Z solar modules
